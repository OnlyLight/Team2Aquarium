Penguin Passport to Love

The Penguin Passport to Love is your ticket to a once-in-a-lifetime experience with SEA LIFE Sydney Aquarium's most romantic and faithful creatures, our sub-Antarctic colony of Gentoo & King Penguins.

Suited up in your snow gear, couples will head out into an icy wonderland where majestic King and cheeky Gentoo penguins waddle, hop, slip and slide along the ice right in front of you. Here you'll observe enrichment activities with the birds and have plenty of opportunities for photos along the way. Couples will also have the opportunity to go behind-the-scenes to see the penguin food preparation area as well as how this state-of-the-art exhibit maintains its chilly temperatures.

Following the on-ice experience you will then enjoy an intimate dinner for two in our penguin viewing area where you can watch the penguins play and swim as they settle down for the evening.

Tickets are strictly limited with a maximum of only 3 couples! So get in quick to secure your spot.

  • This experience is 18+ only; there will be a maximum of 3 couples who will enjoy the penguin passport experience together.

  • No gastro/flu/cold/respiratory symptoms within the last 48 hours of tour

  • Participant must bring their own socks, and not be wearing a dress or skirt
  • Participants must follow instructions of Penguin Trainers at all times
